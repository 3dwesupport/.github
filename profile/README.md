# 3dwesupport

Engineering digital products for real impact.

We build and support products across two brands:

- **BlazeByte** - Product development partner for MVPs, optimization, and team extension.
- **iTinker** - Our non-profit organization focused on helping students become future-ready.

## Our Brands

### BlazeByte
[BlazeByte](https://www.blazebyte.io/) is a Germany-based digital product and engineering partner that supports companies from idea to scale.

What BlazeByte helps teams with:
- **MVP Development**: Rapid prototypes and production-ready MVPs to validate business ideas quickly
- **Product Optimization**: Ongoing, data-driven improvements to UX, performance, and conversion
- **Team Extension**: Dedicated engineers and product support that integrate with in-house teams

Industry experience:
- Healthcare platforms with secure data flows and compliance-aware architecture
- Marketplace systems focused on trust, transactions, and scalable operations
- Ecommerce products optimized for speed, reliability, and conversion
- Gaming and EdTech solutions with engagement-first user experiences

How BlazeByte works:
- Fast, fair, and flexible collaboration model
- Transparent communication with direct team access
- Strong focus on practical delivery and measurable outcomes

### iTinker
[iTinker](https://www.itinker.io/) is our non-profit education initiative working on:
- Skill-building experiences for students
- Curriculum and technology-enabled learning
- Practical, future-ready education workflows
